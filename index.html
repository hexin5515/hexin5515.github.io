About Me

I am a Ph.D in the School of Artificial Intelligence Jilin University, supervised by Professor Xin Wang.

My current research focuses on fairness in Recommender Systems and robustness in Graph Neural Networks.

I aim to develop trustworthy recommendation algorithms and graph neural network architectures that remain reliable, fair, and stable when deployed in real-world, large-scale applications.
